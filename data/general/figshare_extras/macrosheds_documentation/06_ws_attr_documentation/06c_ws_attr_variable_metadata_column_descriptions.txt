MacroSheds watershed attribute variable metadata
Column names and their descriptions


1. variable_code

A brief representation of the name of a MacroSheds watershed attribute. Used ubiquitously to refer to variables within the MacroSheds processing system, package, and dataset.


2. variable_name

The full name of a MacroSheds watershed attribute. Used on macrosheds.org


3. unit

The unit of the variable in the MacroSheds dataset.
